Tour Destinations

Here you can find information about our amazing tour destinations. From exotic beaches to adventurous mountain trails, we have it all covered!

  • Maasai Mara National Reserve: Embark on a safari adventure in one of Africa's most iconic wildlife reserves. Home to the Big Five and the Great Migration, the Maasai Mara promises unforgettable game drives, witnessing the vast savannah teeming with life.
  • Amboseli National Park: Located at the foot of Mount Kilimanjaro, Amboseli offers stunning views of Africa's highest peak along with prolific wildlife, including large herds of elephants, lions, cheetahs, and diverse bird species.
  • Samburu National Reserve: Explore the rugged beauty of Samburu, where the Ewaso Ng'iro River sustains a rich ecosystem. Encounter unique wildlife species such as Grevy's zebras, reticulated giraffes, and Somali ostriches against the backdrop of dramatic landscapes.
  • Lake Nakuru National Park: Discover the flamingo-flecked shores of Lake Nakuru, a haven for birdwatchers and wildlife enthusiasts alike. Marvel at the vast flocks of pink flamingos, as well as rhinos, lions, and other species that inhabit the park.
  • Tsavo National Parks (East and West): Venture into the wild expanse of Tsavo, Kenya's largest national park, divided into East and West. Encounter the legendary "man-eaters of Tsavo," explore lava flows, and witness diverse wildlife against a backdrop of rugged terrain.
  • Lamu Island: Immerse yourself in the tranquility of Lamu, a UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its Swahili culture, pristine beaches, and traditional dhows. Wander through narrow streets lined with ancient architecture, indulge in fresh seafood, and experience the island's rich history.
  • Mount Kenya National Park: Embark on a trekking expedition to the second-highest peak in Africa, Mount Kenya. Whether scaling its snow-capped summit or exploring its lower slopes, adventurers can encounter diverse ecosystems, including equatorial glaciers and dense montane forests.
  • Diani Beach: Relax and unwind on the shores of Diani Beach, voted one of Africa's best beaches. With its crystal-clear waters, powdery white sands, and vibrant coral reefs, Diani offers opportunities for snorkeling, diving, and simply soaking up the sun.
  • Hell's Gate National Park: Experience the unique landscapes of Hell's Gate, characterized by towering cliffs, geothermal features, and diverse wildlife. Visitors can hike, bike, or even rock climb within the park, surrounded by dramatic scenery straight out of a postcard.
  • Nairobi National Park: Escape to the wilderness without leaving the city limits at Nairobi National Park, where skyscrapers provide a backdrop for roaming wildlife. Enjoy game drives amidst a backdrop of acacia forests and open grasslands, with the Nairobi skyline in the distance.

These destinations merely scratch the surface of Kenya's rich tapestry of natural wonders, cultural treasures, and adventurous experiences. From thrilling safaris to serene beach getaways, Kenya offers something for every traveler's taste and preference. We also offer international tours abroad.
